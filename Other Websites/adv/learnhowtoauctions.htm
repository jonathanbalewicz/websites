Step #1, Obtaining the password

In order to make an auction, you'll need to have the password for the guest account. In order to get this password, you'll have to go to http://jzdb.heliohost.org/obtainpassword.htm ; which, there, you must read the contract, which just says that your stuff must be an appropriate auction, and that it must be an auction, and it says that of every bid made with Jz points, we take 75% of the Jz points; then, obtain the password.
